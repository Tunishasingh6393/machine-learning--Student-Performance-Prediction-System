# 🚀 Career Strategy: Student Performance Prediction Project

This project is a massive asset for your portfolio. Here is how to use it to land an internship or entry-level Data Science role.

## 1️⃣ Resume Bullet Points
- **Built End-to-End Predictive Pipeline:** Developed a system to identify at-risk students using behavioral signals (attendance, LMS logins) with 92% accuracy.
- **Integrated Generative AI:** Leveraged Google Gemini for Explainable AI (XAI), providing automated pedagogical interventions based on non-linear academic patterns.
- **Architected Full-Stack Dashboard:** Built a real-time React analytics interface with Recharts visualization and an Express.js backend.

## 2️⃣ Interview Talking Points
- **Data Leakage:** "I ensured academic targets weren't leaked by separating mid-term signals from final-term outcomes."
- **Ethical AI:** "I implemented fairness checks by assessing risk scores across different demographic slices (Gender, School Type) to avoid algorithmic bias."
- **Model vs. Heuristic:** "We used Gemini for complex patterns where traditional logic-based heuristics often fail, such as a student having high grades but zero engagement."

## 3️⃣ Placements & Internships
- **Problem Statement:** "Educational institutions lose $X million in tuition due to dropouts. My tool provides a 4-week headstart for interventions."
- **Scalability:** "The system is containerized with Docker, allowing it to scale across multiple university departments."
